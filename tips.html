Cybersecurity Tips

  • Use Strong Passwords: Create complex passwords that are difficult to guess.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication: Add an extra layer of protection for your accounts.
  • Update Software Regularly: Keep your OS and apps updated to avoid vulnerabilities.
  • Be Cautious with Emails: Avoid clicking on links from unknown sources.
  • Use Antivirus Protection: Install antivirus software to protect against malware.
